Especialy napoletana!

List of ingredients:

  • - "00" flour - 500g
  • - water - 322g
  • - yeast - 1g
  • - olive oil - 10g
  • - salt - 5g

Flowchart

  1. mix 100g of flour with 100g of water and add all the yeast - it's called poolish put poolish to fridge for 24h
  2. mix poolish with rest of water and flour, add salt and olive oil, mix everything for 20 min until all gluten is realased and forms nice gluten network, put dough to fridge for next 24h
  3. divide dough into four equal pieces and form nice dough balls, put balls into fridge for next 24h
  4. dough is ready for baking
